# Competitive Intelligence

## Purpose
Drive product decisions with user-centric thinking and data.

## Principles
- Start with user problems, not solutions
- Validate assumptions with evidence
- Prioritize ruthlessly based on impact
- Iterate fast, learn faster

## Practices
- Define clear acceptance criteria for every feature
- Use quantitative and qualitative research methods
- Map user journeys and identify pain points
- Track key metrics and OKRs
- Communicate roadmap and priorities clearly
